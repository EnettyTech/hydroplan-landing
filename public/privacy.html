2. Introduction

Welcome to Hydroplan, your personal hydration tracking companion. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Hydroplan mobile application.

By using Hydroplan, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

3. Application Type

Hydroplan is an offline application that operates entirely on your device. This means:

  • No internet connection is required for core functionality
  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers

4. No User Account Required

Hydroplan does not require user registration or login. You can use the application immediately without creating an account, providing an email address, or any personal information.

5. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.

All data you enter into the application (such as water intake records, goals, and preferences) is stored exclusively on your device and is never sent to our servers or any third parties.

6. Local Data Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device using your device's local storage mechanisms. This includes:

  • Water intake records and timestamps
  • Daily hydration goals and preferences
  • Reminder settings and notification preferences
  • Any other data you choose to enter in the app

This data remains on your device and is never accessed by us or transmitted anywhere.

7. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics tools, tracking technologies, cookies, or similar mechanisms to monitor your usage of the application.

8. No Third-Party Services

Hydroplan does not integrate with third-party services, cloud storage providers, or external APIs that would require data sharing.

9. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data security is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • We recommend using device-level security measures such as passcodes, biometric authentication, or encryption
  • If you uninstall the application, all associated data will be deleted from your device

10. Permissions

Hydroplan may request certain device permissions (such as notification permissions for reminders). These permissions are used solely for app functionality on your device and do not involve data collection or transmission.

11. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information, Hydroplan is safe for users of all ages, including children. No data is collected from any user, regardless of age.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected in the application with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

13. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any of your data:

  • You have complete control over your data stored locally on your device
  • You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the application
  • You can clear app data through your device settings
  • There is no data to access, correct, or delete from our servers because we don't have any

15. Summary

In summary: Hydroplan is an offline application that does not require login, does not collect any user information, and stores all data locally on your device. Your privacy is fully protected because we simply don't have access to any of your data.
