Privacy Policy - Hydroplan

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Hydroplan, your personal hydration tracking companion. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the Hydroplan mobile application and related services.

By using Hydroplan, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, username, profile picture (if provided)
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials, authentication tokens

2.2 Health and Hydration Data

  • Water Intake Records: Amount of water consumed, timestamps, and daily intake goals
  • Health Goals: Daily hydration targets, personal preferences, and health objectives you set
  • Reminder Settings: Notification preferences and reminder schedules you configure
  • Activity Data: Optional information about physical activity that may affect hydration needs

2.3 Usage Data

  • App Analytics: Features used, session duration, user interactions within the app
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, unique device identifiers
  • Location Data: General location information for timezone settings and regional preferences (not precise location tracking)

2.4 Communication Data

  • Support Requests: Messages sent through customer support channels
  • Feedback: Ratings, reviews, and suggestions provided through the app

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Core Services: Track your daily water intake, generate hydration reports, and send personalized reminders
  • Enhance User Experience: Personalize app features, provide hydration insights, and improve app functionality
  • Communication: Send important updates, reminder notifications, and respond to support requests
  • Analytics: Understand user behavior to improve our services (using aggregated, anonymized data)
  • Security: Protect against unauthorized access and ensure data integrity
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements and respond to legal requests when necessary

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

Hydroplan does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Limited Sharing Scenarios

We may share your information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who help us operate the app (cloud storage, analytics, customer support) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Hydroplan, our users, or others
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)

4.3 Health App Integration

If you choose to integrate with health tracking apps or services:

  • We use read-only access through secure, encrypted connections
  • We partner only with reputable health data providers who comply with health data regulations
  • You can revoke access at any time through app settings
  • We do not share your health data with third parties without your explicit consent

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can view your data within our organization
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure Infrastructure: Data is stored on secure, compliant cloud infrastructure

5.2 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active and as needed to provide services
  • Inactive Accounts: Data may be retained for up to 2 years after account deletion for legal and business purposes
  • Deletion Requests: You can request immediate data deletion (see Section 7)

5.3 International Transfers

If you're located outside our primary data processing region, your data may be transferred to and processed in other countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Hydroplan uses minimal tracking technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for app functionality and security
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage statistics to improve our services
  • No Advertising Cookies: We do not use cookies for advertising or marketing tracking

You can manage cookie preferences through your device settings.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

7.1 Access and Control

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a commonly used format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data

7.2 Communication Preferences

  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications
  • Notification Settings: Control push notifications and reminder alerts

7.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact us at privacy@hydroplan.com or use the in-app privacy settings. We will respond to requests within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Hydroplan is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will:

  • Notify you of significant changes through the app or email
  • Post the updated policy with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Continue to protect your data according to the principles outlined here

10. Regional Privacy Laws

10.1 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information is collected and to opt-out of the sale of personal information.

10.2 European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

European Union users have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to data portability, the right to be forgotten, and the right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities.

10.3 Other Jurisdictions

We comply with applicable privacy laws in all jurisdictions where we operate.

11. Third-Party Services

Hydroplan may integrate with third-party services (health apps, cloud storage, analytics providers). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

12. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours of discovering the breach
  • Provide details about what information was involved
  • Explain steps we're taking to address the breach
  • Offer guidance on protective measures you can take
